# Contributor covenant

This project follows the
[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
Participants are expected to behave respectfully in issues, pull
requests, discussions, and any other community channels associated
with the project.

Unacceptable behaviour can be reported privately to
**conduct@4stm4.dev**. Reports are confidential; the maintainers will
review them and act proportionally (warning, temporary ban, permanent
ban) as outlined in the covenant.

The full text of the covenant is reproduced for convenience at
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/.
